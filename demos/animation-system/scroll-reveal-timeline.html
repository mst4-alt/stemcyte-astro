How Collection Works

Six simple steps from enrollment to preservation

Step 01

Enroll before delivery

Sign up during your pregnancy and receive your collection kit. We'll coordinate with your delivery hospital.

Step 02

Bring your kit to the hospital

When you go into labor, bring the StemCyte collection kit. It contains everything your medical team needs.

Step 03

Collection at birth

After your baby is born and the cord is cut, your doctor collects the cord blood and cord tissue. It's painless and takes about 5 minutes.

Step 04

Shipped to our lab

Our medical courier picks up your collection kit and transports it to our FDA-regulated facility in Baldwin Park, California.

Step 05

Processing & testing

Our lab processes your baby's stem cells, tests for viability and contamination, and prepares them for long-term storage.

Step 06

Cryopreserved for a lifetime

Your baby's stem cells are stored in liquid nitrogen at −196°C, preserved and ready whenever your family needs them.
